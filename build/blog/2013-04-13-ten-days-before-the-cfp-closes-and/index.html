Ten days before the CFP closes and...

There are less than 10 days left before the end of the presentations’ submission period and we thought it would be nice to share some numbers and some feedback of the process so far.

Spoiler: it’s going great and we’re not even halfway there!

Here are some rough, rounded numbers about the process:

  • 52 proposals (3 withdrawn)
  • Close to 200 users
  • 150 suggestions
  • 600 votes
  • 10000 impressions

Here are some comments regarding the process:

Intrigued and impressed by @euruko’s open and social CFP process (@pat_shaughnessy)

I really liked the anonymous approach Euruko was going for (Bamboo)

EuRuKo CfP! This time 10 talks are choosen by the community. Hosting conferences was never easier :-P (@bovensiepen) (*)

(*) No, it’s not!!! See the section below… :P

And here are some number for the application itself:

  • 500+ commits
  • 12 contributors
  • 19 forks of the original Vestibule app
  • At least 5 “fremium” services used to make it be live (more on this on another blog post)

So, what are you waiting for? Send your proposal now!
