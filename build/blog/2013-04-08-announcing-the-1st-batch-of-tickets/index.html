Announcing the 1st batch of tickets!

Hi Rubyists!

After so many months of preparation, finally the wait is over! So mark the date: tickets go on sale on Friday 12th of April, 14:00 UTC through Eventora.

How much?

The ticket price is going to be 60 €. As you may know, EuRuKo has traditionally been a conference with a very reasonable ticket price. The reason is simple: it’s an event organized by the community for the community.

Of course, this wouldn’t have been possible without the love of our sponsors!

How many tickets?

The tickets will be released in batches and the first batch is going to be 100 150 tickets. More will follow of course :-)

So what do I get with a ticket?

Firstly you get to come to a conference where you can decide who is going to speak! In case you haven’t noticed our call for proposals has opened and we already have many awesome presentations. On top of that, you can meet Yukihiro “Matz” Matsumoto, Koichi Sasada and Steve Klabnik who have already confirmed they are going to speak at this year’s EuRuKo. One last thing: you know that you cannot come to Athens and not have a party! That’s all been taken care of by our sponsors :-)

Can’t wait to see you in Athens! Until then, you can watch the countdown :)

UPDATE: We decided to sell 150 tickets due to high demand.
